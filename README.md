# AI lead scoring tool 

## Overview
A lead management automation platform that captures incoming leads from multiple channels, enriches lead data, and facilitates business owner evaluation through automated scoring requests and note collection.

## Problem
Businesses struggle to efficiently process, evaluate, and respond to leads coming from various channels, leading to missed opportunities and inconsistent lead qualification

## Target User
Small to medium business owners and sales teams who receive leads through multiple communication channels

## Features
- Multi-channel lead capture (SMS, email, forms, chat)
- Lead data enrichment and source linking
- Automated lead scoring request generation
- Business owner notification system
- Lead note and scoring collection interface
